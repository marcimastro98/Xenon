What's inside

One screen that does it all

Modular and glanceable — tuned for a touchscreen, flawless with a mouse. Every tile, page, and effect is yours to arrange.

01 / LAYOUT

A Bento grid you actually build

Drag, drop, resize, tab-group, and duplicate widgets across up to 8 pages. A Liquid-Glass redesign with ambient backgrounds and Light / Dark / Auto themes.

drag & drop8 pagestab groupsthemes
02 / INTELLIGENCE

Xenon AI — voice, vision & chat

An assistant that can drive the entire dashboard by voice — and even build a dashboard page for you on request (Genesis). Runs on Google Gemini, or a fully free local provider — Ollama, Whisper, neural TTS — all on your machine.

voice + visionfunction callsGenesislocal or cloud
03 / LIGHT

RGB that reacts to real data

Drive your Corsair / iCUE LEDs from CPU temperature, timers, album art and more — coexisting with iCUE, plus network lights: Govee, LIFX, WLED, Philips Hue and Nanoleaf. Local, key-free. Off by default, lightweight always.

iCUE bridgealbum → LEDGovee · LIFX · Hue
04 / CONTROL

A Stream-Deck in your dashboard

A programmable key grid: launch apps, control media & OBS, fire hotkeys and webhooks, play sounds, or talk to the AI — with folders, multi-action sequences and per-key RGB reactions.

foldersOBS · hotkeyswebhookssoundboard
05 / FOCUS

A lock screen that calms the noise

A distraction-free overlay with an animated clock, now-playing, your next events and the weather — one tap away when you need to lock in.

animated clocknow playingagenda
06 / STREAM

Go live without leaving the dashboard

Twitch, YouTube and OBS widgets — live status, viewers, an editable title, built-in chat, scene switching and one-tap go-live — wired to Deck keys for clips, markers, ads and record.

TwitchYouTubeOBSlive chat
And the rest

Loaded, not bloated

System & Network

CPU, GPU, RAM, disks, throughput, ping — and real in-game FPS via PresentMon.

Media & Audio

Now-playing from any app, album art, device pickers, and a per-app mixer.

Microphone

One-tap mute, live level meter, device picker, and a per-app mic mixer.

Plan your day

Calendar with Outlook / Google .ics sync, tasks, countdown timers, and notes.

Weather

Current conditions, a 3-day forecast and an 8-hour hourly timeline.

Remote PC control

Turn your phone into a remote — Sunshine + Tailscale + Moonlight, orchestrated for you.

Smart AI features

Genesis builds dashboard pages on request; plus Game Companion, Guardian health history (with viewable trend charts) and proactive presence — all opt-in.

Performance & games

Game mode auto-pauses ambient effects in full-screen play; on-demand, fully reversible system optimization.

App switcher

Every open window at a glance — tap to focus — with favourite app shortcuts.

Live browser tile

A real, interactive web page inside the dashboard — tap, scroll and type. Works even with sites that refuse to be framed.

Second screen

A genuine extra Windows desktop inside a tile — drag apps onto it and control it by touch. One-click virtual-display setup.

Updates, in-app

See what’s new in a polished dialog and update in one click — download, verify and restart, with your data preserved.

Field recordings

Every corner, on record

Real screenshots, no mockups. Drag the strip, tap a frame to inspect it full-size.
