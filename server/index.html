      Spotify Queue, playlists & devices

      Connect your Spotify account to add a Spotify tile and Deck keys — the queue, library and devices Windows can't show.

      • See your live Up Next queue with cover art.
      • Browse your playlists and tap one to start it.
      • Switch playback between your Spotify Connect devices.
      • Deck keys: save to Liked, play a playlist, shuffle, switch device.

      Reading the queue, playlists and devices works on any account. Playback control needs Spotify Premium — a Spotify limitation.

      How to connect
      1. Open the Spotify Developer Dashboard and create an app (any name).
      2. In the app settings, add the Redirect URI below (copy it), tick Web API, and save.
      3. Copy the app's Client ID, paste it below, then tap Save.
      4. Tap Connect, approve access in the Spotify tab that opens, and you're linked.
